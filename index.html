WHAT IS
VIA-LANCE?

Via-lance is an “artbook” with a story at its core, created by concept artist, Ozan Kılıç in partnership with Ronin Ventures. The project will be a printed visual novel, and have an online presence as an interactive book where readers will able to own novel scenes as NFTs created on Secret Network.

The plot follows Nine, a female synthetic human who lives in a sci-fi, cyberpunk world. She travels the world with AI-controlled nanomachines in her body, looking for answers to her origins.

FAQ

Ozan Klç developed both the primary tale and the graphics for Via-lance Story. While he specializes in interface design, visual arts, and user experience, he has been working on Via-lance in his spare time for the past several years, and is now collaborating with Ronin Ventures to bring the idea to life.

Ronin Ventures is a company of privacy activists that operate largely on the Secret Network platform. On Secret Network, they are developing privacy-preserving programs that provide unique community experiences. They also provide proof of stake blockchains with network validation services. The technological, commercial, and logistical aspects of Via-Lance are handled by Ronin Ventures.

The Via-Lance NFT collections are a collection of NFTs that depict the VL Story and World. The VL Story NFT collection follows the primary plot and allows collectors to manage the visual components of the interactive book. The content that readers view is completely under the control of the owners. The VL World NFT collection is a precursor to the VL video game, and it builds on the races, tribes, towns, and other aspects of the game.These NFTs will enable unique use cases within the Via-Lance video game.

The protagonist, Nine, is at the center of the plot. A synthetic female human with symbiotic nanomachines living within her body and a central overmind AI managing the atomic nanobots known as R-cells. In a barren world, she seeks answers.

Secret Network is the first blockchain with data privacy by default, allowing you to build and use applications that are both permissionless and privacy-preserving. This unique functionality protects users, secures applications, and unlocks hundreds of new use cases for Web 3.

Secret NFTs are non-fungible tokens but with privacy preserving features of Secret Network. Secret NFTs allow fungibility to be validated without exposing verifiability to the general public. In other words, it is not necessary for verifiable ownership of products and experiences to be made public! The validation process on Secret Network takes place without compromising any sensitive data, including proofs of validity and transfers.
